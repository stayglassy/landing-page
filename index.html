Some random information.
This is a 138" version of a 2021 Ram Promaster. They have a low center of gravity with front wheel drive, making them great for camping off grid locations as well as maneuvering through cities.
Used 5/4 decking boards cut into 1 1/2" strips to frame out the subfloor. Got about 3 strips out of each board. Each strip was glued down and drilled with 2 1/4" #8 self tapping drywall screws.
Used 2x4s for framing out garage and bed area. The shower was a bit of work to level out. Can’t use a level much when the van is constantly shifting around so lots of measurements and a little guessing. 2x4s are the new gold in 2021…
